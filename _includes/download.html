The second release candidate of CWS is ready.

It is tested under WildFly 11 using PostgreSQL. The current version is dubbed 0.9.9, and is the final release candidate. It only awaits that Circle-CI & Travis-CI update their build images to Java 1.8.0_161 or later.

We encourage you to test it, any feedback will be highly welcome, in preparation of the release of the final 1.0 version!
