About CWS


CWS - Cryptographic Web Store - gives you an easy way to store any data safely encrypted.

Data is never persisted unencrypted and only minimal necessary metadata is stored.

Support for circles of trust allows to share encrypted data amongst a group of people.

KeyManagement is important, and all keys are controlled by the members directly.

We have close to 100% test coverage.
